Exposur3.io

Exposur3 is a personal project designed to be a simple notification tool that helps introduce and refresh knowledge from various topics. This personal project was started to help remember various commands, concepts, excerpts, etc. from a variety of sources. After initial use and experimentation, the value of exposing seemingly random topics to people within different technical industries was more apparent. Wide range of knowledge or exposure to concepts can be invaluable when approaching holistic approaches to software development, project management, cybersecurity, and many other fields that many specialize in.



Threshodl Crypto-Wallet

Threshodl was the embodiment of an off-blockchain transaction concept that allowed cryptocurrencies to be transferred off-chain through the use of "micro-wallets". During May 2018, this was patent-pending under the application number 15/978,008. Due to the dissolving of the company (ThreeBx), efforts to secure patent protection were stopped. As CTO of ThreeBx, I developed this unique patent along with the first beta version of the mobile application and corresponding backend services.



SharkTrap.com

Shark Trap is developing a line of "super shufflers" designed to bring intelligence and optimization into table games. At SharkTrap, I have worked as a technical advisor to help develop algorithms for marked card detection, hashing and randomization algorithms, and various computer vision functions.
